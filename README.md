# Pythia

Pythia is an open-source vocal synthesizer built for music students who do not want to use their real voice, do not have time to record, or simply enjoy the vocal synth format. Pythia aims to be the leading vocal synthesizer in the education space — accessible, expressive, and free.

---

## What is Pythia?

Pythia gives students a synthetic voice they can compose with. Whether you are writing your first song or producing a full track, Pythia provides a voice without the barriers of recording equipment, vocal ability, or studio time.

It is designed to be simple enough for a complete beginner while remaining a legitimate creative tool.

---

## Voicebanks

Pythia ships with a growing roster of voicebanks, each with a distinct sound and character.

**Mary** — The default voicebank. Mary is a robot built by Lilian, who she calls her mother. She was created not as an experiment or a project, but as a solution to loneliness — and she became something far greater than either of them expected. Warm and earnest, Mary is the heart of the Pythia roster and the first voice most users will ever hear.

**Lilian** — The first English voicebank and Mary's creator. Lilian is a coder who shares a home with her sister Ray. Though she has family around her, a particular kind of loneliness led her to build Mary — and in doing so, she found the daughter she did not know she was looking for. She treats Mary with a love that not everyone around her understands. Currently unreleased.

**Aria** — Voiced by a real six year old. Aria is bright, chaotic, and entirely herself — a pick-me younger sister whose siblings are perpetually exhausted by her, even as they quietly adore her. Her voicebank is living proof that Pythia is simple enough for anyone to use.

**StrawberryChan** — A work in progress. StrawberryChan was born with magical girl powers so vast they are almost incomprehensible — and she has chosen to keep them almost entirely hidden, not wanting her younger sister BlueberryChan to feel lesser by comparison. These days she works as a maid at a grand mansion, using roughly 0.1% of her abilities to be the finest maid anyone has ever seen. On her days off she cheers enthusiastically at her master's baseball games. Her voicebank is sampled from a strawberry and vocoded into Japanese phonetics, and sounds exactly as strange and wonderful as that implies.

**Ray** — Lilian's sister, and the most quietly devastating voice in the roster. Ray has been through a great deal — bad friends, manipulation, depression — and her voicebank carries all of it. She is being released as someone who has moved on. Not healed in a neat or simple way, but through it, and still standing. Currently unreleased.

The Pythia universe is ongoing. As new voicebanks are developed and released, the world and its characters will continue to expand.

## File Formats

Pythia uses its own file formats across the ecosystem.

**.pys** — Pythia Song. The project file format. Saves your composition, notes, lyrics, tempo, and voicebank references so you can pick up where you left off.

**.pvb** — Pythia Voice Bank. The compiled voicebank format used by the engine at runtime.

**.pyai** — The source voicebank format. Used to author and edit voicebanks before converting them to .pvb for use in the program.

## Installation

Pythia requires Python and two dependencies.

**Dependencies**

- numpy
- PyQt6

Installation guides for both are included in the repository. If you have Python installed, you can be up and running in under ten minutes.
